ADMISSION REQUIREMENTS

The minimum requirement for admission or general entry to our esteemed institution, especially for applicants in Ghana, is WASSCE/SSSCE certificates.

WASSCE

An applicant for admission must have at least credits (A1 - C6 in WASSCE) in English, Core Mathematics and Integrated Science or Social Studies and three elective subjects.

SSSCE

An applicant for admission must have at least credits (A – D in SSSCE) in English, Core Mathematics and Integrated Science or Social Studies and three elective subjects.

OTHER

Applicants with certificates equal to or higher than the WASSCE/SSSCE, such as; IGCSE or IB should visit our main campus at Ofankor Barrier, Accra with their results and certificates.
