Know Something About Us

We are the Best Health College in Ghana

Do you want to become a Pharmacy Assistant (Medicine Counter Assistant, MCA)? Then look no further than Alliance Health College. Alliance Health College is an educational institution, founded on July 8, 2020 to provide students with certain vocational skills and knowledge in medicine dispensery and healthcare. We invite students from within the nation and beyond to study in any one of our professional healthcare programs, like our six month Medicine Counter Assistant (MCA) training. Upon completion, we award a certificate that enables you to work in a Pharmacy as a support staff. We also offer have a diploma in Pharmacy program, as well as various programs in Salesmanship & Marketing in pharmacy.

We also provide our students with guaranteed Attachment opportunities to help them acquire and apply knowledge, skills and feelings in an immediate and relevant setting through the engagement of the body and mind through activity, reflection and application which tends to provide depth and meaning to a learning/training system and make it easy for them to get a job as well.

We are also aware that financial contraints more often than not, deny bright students certain opportunities to study and/or hone their skills. Which why we have flexible payment Plans, to allow students who are unable to pay their tuiton fees immediately.
