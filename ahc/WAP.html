Website Accessibility Policy

At Alliance Health College, we are committed to ensuring digital accessibility for people with disabilities. We are continually improving the user experience for everyone and applying relevant accessibility standards.

Our Commitment

We are dedicated to meeting accessibility requirements for our website and ensuring that all users have equal access to our online resources. Our goal is to provide a seamless and accessible experience for all visitors, regardless of their abilities or impairments.

Accessibility Features

To enhance user experience, our website incorporates various accessibility features, including but not limited to:

  • Keyboard navigation support
  • Text-to-speech compatibility
  • Contrast and color adjustments
  • Accessible forms and buttons

Feedback and Support

We welcome feedback on the accessibility of our website. If you encounter any accessibility issues or have specific concerns, please contact us using the information provided below.
