Admissions at Alliance Health College

Welcome to our admissions page. We are excited about your interest in joining Alliance Health College, where we empower students to achieve their academic and professional goals.At Alliance Health College, we are dedicated to fostering a supportive and enriching educational experience for our students. Whether you're a prospective student, parent, or guardian, this page will guide you through our admission process.

Application Process

To apply to Alliance Health College, follow these steps:

  1. - Complete the online application form.
  2. - Submit required documents, including transcripts and references.
  3. - Attend an admissions interview (if applicable).
  4. - Receive notification of acceptance.
  5. - Complete enrollment formalities.

Eligibility Criteria

  • - Minimum GPA of 3.0 in previous academic studies.
  • - Strong recommendation letters from educators or professionals.
  • - Passing score on the college entrance examination.
  • - Fluency in English (for international students).
  • - Age: Students must meet the age requirements for the desired grade level.
  • - Academic Qualifications: Provide details about any specific academic prerequisites.
  • - Documents: List the necessary documents (birth certificate, transcripts, etc.)

Admission Requirements

The minimum requirement for admission or general entry to our esteemed institution, especially for applicants in Ghana, is WASSCE/SSSCE certificates.

  • - An applicant for admission must have at least credits (A – D in SSSCE) in English, Core Mathematics and Integrated Science or Social Studies and three elective subjects.

  • - An applicant for admission must have at least credits (A1 - C6 in WASSCE) in English, Core Mathematics and Integrated Science or Social Studies and three elective subjects.

  • - Applicants with certificates equal to or higher than the WASSCE/SSSCE, such as; IGCSE or IB should visit our main campus at Ofankor Barrier, Accra with their results and certificates.
